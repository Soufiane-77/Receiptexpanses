Receipts for freelancers: staying ready for tax season

May 12, 2026 · The ReceiptExpenses Team · 5 min read

Freelance income often arrives in messy ways — a cash payment here, a bank transfer there. Consistent receipts turn that mess into a record you can stand behind.

Three habits that pay off

  • Issue a receipt for every sale, even small cash ones
  • Use sequential receipt numbers so nothing looks skipped
  • Save a copy the moment you create it — don't rely on memory
